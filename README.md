## Company Overview

We help organizations protect their people and protect their bottom line—without turning the job into a paperwork contest.

Northline Safety Solutions provides risk management, safety, health, and emergency response consulting for construction, industrial, and operational environments. We live in the real world: tight schedules, changing conditions, high-energy hazards, and crews that need clear direction more than another binder.

Our work is delivered on a scoped, contract basis, and built to travel—focused on protecting people, reducing risk, and keeping the job moving. If you’re trying to keep your recordables down or make sure your response capability actually works when something goes sideways, this is exactly what we do.

## Core Competencies

### Construction & Industrial Support  
*Strengthening safety performance on the floor and in the field.*

- **Jobsite & Plant Leadership:** Presence that raises the standard with minimal slowing of production.  
- **High-Risk Work Planning:** Energized work, LOTO, confined space, lifting/rigging, and SIMOPS.  
- **Program Builds & Tune-Ups:** Internal consulting services.  
- **Incident Investigations:** Root cause analysis that produces solutions crews can actually execute.  
- **Mock OSHA Audits:** Pressure-testing your documentation and field behavior before an inspector does.  
- **Regulatory Support:** Organizing facts, coaching leadership, and tightening document flow to reduce unforced errors during inspections.  
- **Training That Lands:** Custom-built for supervisors and crews based on their current level (no death-by-PowerPoint).  
- **Standby Rescue:** Real capability and accountability for confined space, work at height, and shutdowns.
